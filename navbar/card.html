Hunza Valley

The Hunza Valley (Burushaski: ہنزو, Wakhi: shina; Urdu: ہنزہ) is a mountainous valley in the northern part of the Gilgit-Baltistan region of Pakistan, formed by the Hunza River, bordering Ishkoman to the northwest, Shigar to the southeast, Afghanistan's Wakhan Corridor to the north, and the Xinjiang region of China to the northeast
